
Our grow tent provides a highly efficient, semi enclosed (mesh), controllable environment that maximizes plant growth while saving space, water and electricity. It also has the added benefit of creating its own micro biome for optimal soil fertility. It is specifically designed to take the harsh conditions of the South African climate in mind. With the added benefit of retrofitting your own Perma Dome to accomadte the micro-climate and soil conditions required for growing any fruit, vegetables, mushrooms, herbs and spices directly where you live.
